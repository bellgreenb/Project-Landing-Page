Some stuff about Plants

There is a wide variety of plants that exist, even among members of its own species. For instance, there are thousands of different varieties of apples, a few of which are consumed in the US. The same applies with tomatoes, peppers, strawberries, and more. The advantage of having a garden is that you can grow varieties of your favorite fruits and vegetables that are almost never seen in regular supermarkets. This can serve as an opportunity to greatly expand your pallet, and learn how to become more self-sufficient.
